About Saeedeh

1/6 My Resume

Beautiful Art Is a Process

Tayebeh Keshvari known as “Saeedeh” which means happiness is a Persian artist & designer.

She is a graduate of Fine Arts & Interior Design from Regent’s university London. Her work is inspired by Persian & Arabic literature such as Rumi’s poetry and other Middle Eastern poets and scholars.

My art is more than just showing beautiful pictures. They deliver a message that creates a spiritual shift within the viewer by touching their hearts

3/6Source of Inspiration

Behind The Creation



Literature and poetry is the source of inspiration for her creations. Rumi’s poems, the Persian poet of 13 century is one of the main inspiration of her art, you can find other Middle Eastern poets & philosophers words in her work such as Gibran Khalil Gibran & Mohammad Darvish.


Saeedeh’s art pieces has been sold worldwide & went to hundreds of new home of art collections in cities such as Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Jeddah, Riyadh, London, Paris, Madrid, Toronto, Vancouver, San Francisco, Los Angels, etc.
